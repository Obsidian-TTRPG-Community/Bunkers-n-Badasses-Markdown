> [!skill] Entropic Blast
> **Tree**:: [[Compendium/BnB/Sourcebook Homebrews/Creating a Vault Hunter/The Classes/Siren/Siren of Death/Siren of Death|Siren of Death]]

> **Mayhem**:: 1 Point
> **Obtained**:: Level 1
> **Skill-Type**:: Martial
