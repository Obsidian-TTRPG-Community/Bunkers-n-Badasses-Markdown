# Rage of the Ancients
___Requires:___ [Brr-Zerker](Brr-Zerker.md)
After activating your Action Skill, become _Enraged_ for 2 turns.
While _Enraged_, add +1d6 Cryo Damage to all of your Ranged and Melee Attacks.
