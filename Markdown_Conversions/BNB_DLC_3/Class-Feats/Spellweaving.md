# Spellweaving

***Requires:*** [Spellshot](../Classes/Siren/Spellshot.md)

Tossing a Grenade or Reloading grants you a point of Spellweaving, up to 5, until the end of the Day.
Gain +1 Grenade Damage for each point of Spellweaving.
