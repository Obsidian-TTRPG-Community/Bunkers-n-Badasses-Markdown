## Starting Gold

**Step 1**: get gold.
**Step 2**: repeat step 1.

Each Vault Hunter determines their starting gold by rolling 1d6/Level. Each d6 will grant the amount of gold listed in the table below.

**Starting Gold Table**

| `dice: 1d6` | Gold |
| ----------- | ---- |
| 1           | 30g  |
| 2           | 50g  |
| 3           | 80g  |
| 4           | 100g |
| 5           | 120g |
| 6           | 150g |
